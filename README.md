# 📊 Superstore Analytics Dashboard

## Overview

The Superstore Analytics Dashboard is an interactive data analytics project built using Python and Streamlit. The project analyzes retail sales data to uncover business insights related to sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, regional performance, and discount impact.

The dashboard enables users to explore key business metrics through interactive visualizations and actionable recommendations.

---

## Project Objectives

* Analyze sales and profit performance.
* Identify top-performing and underperforming product categories.
* Evaluate regional and state-level profitability.
* Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
* Study the impact of discounts on profit.
* Provide business recommendations based on data-driven insights.

## Key Insights

* Technology generated the highest sales and profit.
* Copiers were the most profitable sub-category.
* Tables and Bookcases generated losses.
* West Region achieved the highest profit margin.
* California and New York were the most profitable states.
* Texas generated the highest loss.
* High discounts often reduced profitability.

---

## Business Recommendations

1. Reduce excessive discounting on low-margin products.
2. Improve profitability of Tables and Bookcases.
3. Focus on high-profit products such as Copiers.
4. Investigate losses in underperforming states.
5. Strengthen retention strategies for profitable customers.
